# BGnius VITA Brand Guidelines

## Logo
- Min size: 24x24
- App icon: 24x24
- Menu: 24x24, white bg or transparent
- NO: change colors, deform, rotate

## Colors

### Logo Colors
- Purple: #9D4B8F
- Blue: #0072BC
- White: #FFFFFF

### Primary
- Purple: #7B2CBF
- Blue: #0072BC
- Dark Blue: #303F9F

### Secondary (Purple scale)
- #F2EAF9, #D6BEEB, #5C218F, #2B0F43, #7B2CBF

### Transparency (Purple)
- #F0EBF8, #D0C2E9, #3E236E, #241440

### Transparency (Blue)
- #E6F1F8, #B0D3EA, #00568D, #002842

### Transparency (Dark Blue)
- #E6F1F8, #BFC3E1, #1D265F, #111638

### Neutrals
- #FFFFFF, #F5F5F5, #F3EFEF, #E0E0E0, #757575, #BFC3E1, #212121

### States
- Success/Open: #4CAF50
- Info: #2196F3
- Pause: #9E9E9E
- Close: #FF5722
- Error: #E53935
- Warning: #FF9800

## Typography
- Font: **Montserrat**
- Titles: Bold
- Subtitles: Bold or SemiBold
- Paragraphs: Regular

## Visual Style
- Icons: Simple lines (NO emojis)
- Illustrations: Simple with soft colors
- Use Material Icons or similar line-icon library
